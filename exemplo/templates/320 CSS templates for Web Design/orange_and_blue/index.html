Welcome to Blue&Orange

Blue&Orange is my first attempt at a template for OSWD and I hope people like it, It is a static 1 column layout designed to meet xhtml and css standards. I originally used this design, albeit in a table layout, for my current web site, visual-aid.co.uk (currently undergoing content change). It is actually a mix of my original table design and various other designs including leaves by SmallPark (look at the header text). This is my first practice at using css to layout a page and I am happy enough that the simplicity (which I think is missing from the majority of sites) and easily recognizable navigation work well. The site works well down to 800x600, and, because it is centered will continue to work into the higher resolutions. I have one restriction on using this template, please keep the design by brampamp in the footer and email me if you use this template with a link to the site. Other than that please feel free to massacre it and make it yours!
