Obligatory Quote From Nietzsche

In every template (except for one), I've added a quote from Nietzsche, someone that may not be well known by most people. Friedrich Nietzsche was a German philosopher who died in 1900. He is famous for many concepts; he was quite the thinker, and often is hard to understand in his writings. Still, he is informative (and often makes a funny remark in the process).

On the rare occasions when our dreams succeed and achieve perfection—most dreams are bungled—they are symbolic chains of scenes and images in place of a narrative poetic language; they circumscribe our experiences or expectations or situations with such poetic boldness and decisiveness that in the morning we are always amazed at ourselves when we remember our dreams. We use up too much artistry in our dreams—and therefore often are impoverished during the day.
- Nietzsche, The Wanderer and His Shadow
