Extra column

3-column layout

If you like, you can put an extra column on the right side. Some people think this makes your website too cluttered, and I tend to agree -- but at least you have the option.

Your right column can contain anything that the main content column does.

  • Directions to your house
  • Your favorite soup recipe
  • Information about products or services

Welcome to Pistachio

Paragraph styles

This template was inspired by all of my left-over Pistachios from Christmas... I love nuts of every kind, but Pistachios are awesome. Regular paragraphs are indented by 25px on the first line.

Pistachio is 100% valid XHTML-Strict and CSS. This paragraph shows how you can automatically make the first letter in every paragraph look just like the beginning of a chapter in a book.
