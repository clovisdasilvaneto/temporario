Intro

Welcome to ::equanimity::
This site has a 3 column layout with the main content in the middle. It could be used as a personal page, blog, or even a business site.

The idea

The word "equanimity" means 'steadiness of mind under stress'. This gave me the idea for a peaceful, green design, with a pleasant balance between the colours.

About this site

This site is marked up in XHTML Strict and uses CSS for the formatting. It is meant to look calm and peaceful. The colour scheme is green & light grey, fits together well and is easy on the eyes.

The top navigation is 'tabbed', showing the visitor where they are and where they can go. The right and left-hand sides are columns, but if their content exceeds the main content in the middle, then a new effect is created, with the middle becoming an island on the grey.
This site is compatible with multiple viewing resolutions, even when the font is resized.
