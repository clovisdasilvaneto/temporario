6、I Love Children

Children are lovely! Children are angels.

And I want to introduce 1kg More.

1kg More (short for “1 kilogram more in your travel pack”) is a non-official cause of charity crossing over travel.

It runs behind the idea of travelers bringing 1kg of inexpensive educational materials in addition to their package to give away to some one that needs it along their journey, e.g. kids that cannot even afford a pencil rubber.

The simple process of passing-communicating-sharing will no doubt enrich travelers’ travel experience and realize the positive cycle of charity work.
