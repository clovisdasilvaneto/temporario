This is my 4th submission to OSWD, and while it's not my personal favorite, I do like it. The design is valid XHTML 1.1, but unfortunatly due to the opacity hacks for Internet Explorer, the CSS does not validate. That being said, the page does display properly in both Firefox and IE, and everything but the hacks do validate. The background patterns are from Squidfingers and are free to use. The background image is from Stock.Xchng and is also free to use. As always, this template is completely open source, and you may use it as you wish, just please keep a link back to me on it. If you need anyhelp modifying the template you can email me at brett7481@msn.com.
