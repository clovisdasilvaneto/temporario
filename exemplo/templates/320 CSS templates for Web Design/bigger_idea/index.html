About this design

This is a modification of the Big Idea design—with the content area centred on the page with a border. I initially thought of modifying the original design, but then realized that they look different, and some people might prefer one over the other. The image of the bulb at the top is taken from sxc.hu and there are no restrictions on its use.

Of course, there are no restrictions on the use of this design either, though I'll be grateful if you keep the credit line. If possible, do drop me a mail if you use this. It's always interesting to see how my designs have been used and improved upon.

About me

I'm a freelance editor, IT/sports writer and author, not to mention an amateur web designer. I'm a relatively new entrant to CSS-based design, but I shudder to think of life without CSS! My own websites are WRITEside.net and PayalDhar.com.
