About this design

This a fixed-width single-column design—sorry, I know that's obvious, but I'm trying to fill in text to show what it'll look like! The image of the bulb at the top is taken from sxc.hu and there are no restrictions on its use.

The design was make primarily for a 1024x768 pixel resolution, thought it looks all right on 1280x1024 as well. For a higher or lower resolution, you may have to tinker with the positioning of the header text next to the bulb image. (If anyone has an idea how to fix this problem across resolutions, do let me know.)

Of course, feel free to use and modify the design as you deem fit. Do drop me a line if you can if you use or modify this template. It's always nice to see how my designs have been used and improved upon.

I'm a freelance editor, IT/sports writer and author, not to mention an amateur web designer. I'm a relatively new entrant to CSS-based design, and I shudder to think of life without CSS! My own websites are WRITEside.net and PayalDhar.com.
