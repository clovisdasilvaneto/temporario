My First Submission

posted by defproc on 8th March 2006, 13 comments
This is my first CSS-based design and my first submission to oswd. I'm submitting it as a sort of 'thank you' to the oswd community for opening my eyes to the power of CSS. Sure I've used it before, for colouring this and that and applying style to my table cells, and I knew it could do the things it does on this page, but I underestimated its power until I came across oswd. Consider me enlightened.
